Our Vision

To become the leading examination management system across educational institutions in Sri Lanka, fostering academic excellence through innovative technology solutions that simplify content management and enable students to achieve their full potential in Advanced Level examinations.

Comprehensive Paper Management Platform

ALHORIZON is a sophisticated digital ecosystem designed to streamline the entire lifecycle of A/L examination resources, from upload and organization to distribution and accessibility.

Centralized Repository

A unified digital library housing thousands of past papers, MCQ collections, marking schemes, and model answers organized by subject, year, and paper type.

Secure & Encrypted

Industry-standard encryption protocols ensure all examination materials remain confidential and protected from unauthorized access or distribution.

Role-Based Access

Customized permissions for administrators, teachers, and students ensure appropriate access levels while maintaining system security and integrity.

Advanced Analytics

Comprehensive usage statistics and insights help administrators track resource utilization and identify areas for improvement.

Exam Scheduling

Automated calendar integration with exam dates, deadlines, and reminders keeps everyone informed about upcoming assessments.

Multi-Stream Support

Comprehensive coverage of all A/L streams including Physical Science, Biological Science, Commerce, Arts, and Technology subjects.
